Jalapeno Poppers

Description

"Description of the recipe shown above."

Ingrediants

  • Jalapenos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the jalapenos in half and devein them. Once done set them aside
  2. Cut the bacon into small pieces and in a pan and cook the bacon on medium heat
  3. In a large bowl mix together the cream cheese, cooked bacon, and shredded cheese
  4. With a spoon, spread the cream cheese mixture onto the jalapeno halves
  5. Cook in the air fryer at 375 for 15 minutes
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions or dip them in sour cream and enjoy
